Field recordings- under heavy construction

Hiking and spending time outdoors are some of my favorite hobbies, and one of the ways I like to commemorate my time outside is by taking audio recordings of my natural surroundings when I'm out. I created the map below to keep track of all the places I've recorded. I'm slowly adding MP3 files to the map pop-ups, so please bear with.
I'm currently recording on a Zoom H5 digital recorder onto Lexar 1000x 32GB SD cards, with the Sony MDR7506 Professional headphones.
