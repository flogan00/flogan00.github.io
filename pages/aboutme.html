Biography

I am a graduate student at the University of Northern Colorado (UNCO) studying Sport Administration in the School of Sport and Exercise Science. I am a graduate assistant at the Marcus Garvey Cultural Center, and also work for different staff and faculty as a research assistant from time to time. In 2023, I was voted "Most likely to win the Hunger Games" by the Garvey staff, so take that as you will.

In life, I want to be an educator and researcher, and work as a consultant to help municipalities implement sport development projects while preserving residential communities and local businesses that might be negatively impacted by the project.

In my spare time, I love to hike, play video games, read, and work on my beloved car. I also study different languages. I speak, read, and write Czech at an intermediate level, and am beginning my Finnish journey :)
