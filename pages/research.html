    • Current project

    I am evaluating instances of MLB fans on Twitter expressing ownership over players at the players’ expense, specifically regarding Clayton Kershaw’s aborted perfect game in 2022. I’m also demonstrating why fans have that attitude, which boils down to analyzing consumer behavior and how professional teams encourage it. I am using a combination of quantitative Tweet analysis, articles about Kershaw’s game, and older articles displaying consumer capitalism in MLB to conduct my research.


    Researching style

    Archival research is very much within my wheelhouse. I'm trying to incorporate a more sociological approach to research and writing and have been working on IRBs.
    I'm a firm believer in academic accessibility, so I do my best to make my presentations and papers as friendly to the widest audience I can through detailed explanations and references. Accesibility is my number one goal as a junior scholar.
