About Frankie


I am currently an undergraduate student at the University of New Mexico, double majoring in Classical Studies (BA) and Geography (BS). I am a Mellon Mays Undergraduate Fellowship (MMUF) fellow and an intern for UNM Baseball in their analytics department. When I'm not at work, studying, or completing scouting reports, I enjoy watching sports (Go Cubs; go Red Wings), translating Ancient Greek literature, and writing for fun.


Research


INTERESTS: As I mentioned before, I am an MMUF fellow, where I conduct research about labor issues and the analytic revolution in youth baseball organizations, specifically the Amateur Athletic Union (AAU), and Little League Baseball (LLB). I am also interested in the changes in politics, specifically neoliberalism, within sports broadcasting from the 1970s to 2020s.

PLANS: In graduate school next year, I would like to start researching neoliberal communication and sports broadcasting. I anticipate writing my master's thesis on the intersection of AAU baseball, multi-billion-dollar American corporations, and labor.

Reading List


Here is a small list of some of my favorite reads throughout my experience as a junior scholar so far. All of them are what I would consider to be cornerstones of my research and would highly suggest to anyone interested in the same topics as me. Happy reading!

  • Carriere, M. H. “‘A Diamond Is a Boy’s Best Friend’: The Rise of Little League Baseball, 1939-1964.” Journal of Sport History 32, no. 3 (2005): 351–78.
  • Carrington, B., and McDonald, I. eds. Marxism, Cultural Studies and Sport. Routledge Critical Studies in Sport. Abingdon ; New York: Routledge, 2009.
  • Helyar, J. Lords of the Realm. Ballantine Books, 1995.
  • hooks, b. Teaching to Transgress: Education as the Practice of Freedom. New York: Routledge, 1994.
  • Marx, K. “The Division of Labor and Manufacture.” In Capital, translated by Ben Fowkes, 4th ed., 1:455–91. Penguin Books, 1976.
  • McPherson, J. B.. The Conservative Resurgence and the Press: The Media’s Role in the Rise of the Right. Medill School of Journalism Visions of the American Press. Evanston, Ill: Northwestern University Press, 2008.
  • Passan, J. “Inside the Self-Inflicted Crisis Boiling over as MLB’s Lockout Deadline Arrives.” ESPN.com, February 28, 2022. Link.
  • Sanderson, J., and Baerg, A. “Youth Baseball and Data Analytics: Quantifying Risk Management and Producing Neoliberal Responsible Citizenship Through the GameChanger App.” Communication & Sport 8, no. 1 (February 2020): 72–91.
