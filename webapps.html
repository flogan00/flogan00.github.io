Riparian vegetation along the Rio Grande Corridor over time

This project is an analysis of vegetation density along the Rio Grande in New Mexico, USA. I worked with Katie Slack and Emily Smith for Dr. Liping Yang's Internet Mapping course at the University of New Mexico. The link to my groupmate's site is down, so please excuse this entry as I work on locally storing the files.





The Stadium/Arena Monitoring Project (StAMP)

This web application is a community resource for learning more about publicly subsidized professional sports stadiums and arenas and their impacts on their host cities. It is intended to teach and help voters make informed decisions from someone who has nothing to gain financially from whether they approve or deny the use of public funds to build a new pro stadium. It is an interactive map with some open-access resources provided, as well as a satellite timeline. I created this application as a result of my master's thesis, and am hoping to continue refining this model during my doctoral studies. Here is the link to this young, ongoing project.
