About me

My background

  • M.S. in Sport Administration University of Northern Colorado (expected 2024)
  • B.S. in Geography, B.A. in Classical Studies (Ancient Greek) University of New Mexico

Biography

I am a graduate student at the University of Northern Colorado (UNCO) studying Sport Administration in the School of Sport and Exercise Science. I am a graduate assistant at the Marcus Garvey Cultural Center, and also work for different staff and faculty as a research assistant from time to time. In 2023, I was voted "Most likely to win the Hunger Games" by the Garvey staff, so take that as you will.

In life, I want to be an educator and researcher, and work as a consultant to help municipalities implement sport development projects while preserving residential communities and local businesses that might be negatively impacted by the project.


Extracurricular interests

I'm a really big sports fan, of course. I follow baseball, ice hockey, and motorsports, mostly Formula 1, World Rally Championship, and World Edurance Championship.

In my spare time, I love to hike, play video games, read, and work on my beloved car. I speak, read, and write Czech at an intermediate level, and use that power only for evil.

My greatest fear is opening those biscuit canisters and my greatest dream is to be able to pass on my best experiences in education to everyone I can.
